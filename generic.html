a little bit about me

My Name is Francisco (Frank) Guadarrama and my journey in data analysis began when my boss asked me if I could build a dashboard. Although I had never built one before, I was up for the challenge and excited to learn something new.

As I worked on the project, I discovered a passion for exploring data and uncovering insights. I began to experiment with different visualization techniques, learning about data analysis, and gradually building my skills through trial and error. This experience inspired me to pursue further learning opportunities in the field, and I eventually became a dedicated data analyst, eager to continue using data to solve real-world problems.

What I find most interesting about data is its ability to tell a story. Every dataset has a unique narrative, and as a data analyst, my role is to extract meaningful insights from it. I love the challenge of working with large datasets and finding patterns that may not be immediately obvious. I am constantly fascinated by the new insights that data can provide and the impact it can have on decision-making.

My passion lies in using data to solve real-world problems. I believe that data analytics has the potential to transform the way businesses operate and improve the lives of people worldwide. I enjoy working with a diverse range of data, from customer data to financial data, and am always eager to learn more about different industries and domains. Ultimately, my goal is to use my skills in data analysis to make a positive impact on the world around me.
